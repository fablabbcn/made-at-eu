Indentifying metrics

How does a city work? What does it need to function?

Energy production/consumption

How much energy does a city need? Where does it come from? Encouraging the local production and storage of energy. Developing the Internet of Energy.

Food production/consumption

Where do our supplies come from? Can we think on empty office buildings and stadiums as massive urban farms? Microfarming at home, synthetic biology and superfoods.

From PITO to DIDO

How many shipping containers does a city need? Where do they come from? From Product In > Trash Out (PITO) to Data In < > Data Out (DIDO). Data travels between cities and not goods.
